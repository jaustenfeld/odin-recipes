Chickpea salad

A great fresh tasting and protein dense salad to enjoy for lunch or as a side dish for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • One bell pepper
  • 3-4 medium sized shallots
  • 1 medium sized cucumber
  • Fresh parsley
  • Fresh mint
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 lemon
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron

Steps

  1. Strain and rinse chickpeas
  2. Dice all vegetables
  3. Finely chop fresh herbs
  4. Juice one lemon and combine with olive oil to create the dressing
  5. Combine chickpeas, vegetables, herbs, and dressing in a serving bowl
  6. Salt to taste
  7. Mix in 1-2 spoonfuls of saffron

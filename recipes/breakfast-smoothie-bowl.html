Breakfast Smoothie Bowl

Delicious and nutrient packed smoothie bowl to start your day with.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Frozen mixed berries
  • 1 spoonful seamoss (blended and hydrated)
  • 1 spoonful spirulina
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 spoonful honey
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Oat milk
  • Oats, fresh fruit, and dark chocolate chips as garnish

Steps

  1. Add frozen berries to blender
  2. Add seamoss, spirulina, protein powder, avocado, honey, and turmeric to blender
  3. Add oatmilk to cover contents of blender
  4. Optional: Add ice cubes to blender to decrease temperature of smoothie
  5. Blend smoothie and pour into a bowl
  6. Add desired garnishes to smoothie bowl

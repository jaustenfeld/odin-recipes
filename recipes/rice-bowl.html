Rice Bowl

Tasty and filling dinner to satisfy hunger.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 handful arugula
  • frozen (or fresh) edamame
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Everything but the bagel seasoning
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 egg, fried
  • Pickled red onions

Sauce

  • 2 parts soy sauce, one part rice vinegar, sesame oil to taste, 1 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Begin cooking rice either in a rice cooker or on the stove
  2. Dice carrot and sautee on medium heat
  3. Dice garlic and add with edamame and chili flakes to pan
  4. Let carrots and edamame continue to sautee for 3-4mins while preparing cucumber and avocado
  5. When rice is ready, fill 1/2 of a serving bowl, filling the other half with arugula
  6. Add cooked carrots and edamame on top of rice, season with everything but the bagel seasoning
  7. Arrange cucumber, avocado, and pickled red onion on top of arugula
  8. Fry one egg, while egg is frying prepare sauce
  9. Pour sauce over carrots, edamame, and rice, and place the fried egg on top
  10. Adjust seasoning to personal preferences and serve
